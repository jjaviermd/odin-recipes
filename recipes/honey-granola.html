Honey Peanut Granola

Healy hipocaloric granola in just one hour

This sweet, crunchy homemade granola combines quick oats with peanuts, wheat germ,brown sugar and vanilla.Baked in the oven , it's easy to make


ingredients

  • 3 cups quick cooking oats
  • 1/2 cupchopedpeanuts
  • 1/4 cup wheat germ
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/3 cupbrown sugar
  • 1/4 cup vegetabe oil
  • 2 tablespoon warm water
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon valilla stract

directions

  1. Preheat oven at 250 degrees F (120 degrees C). lightly grease a baking sheet
  2. in a large bowl combine oats, peanuts an wheat germ
  3. In a separete bowl combine, honey, brown sugar, vegetable oil, water, salt and vanilla. stir well; then pour into the oat mixture, and stir. spread out on a cookie sheet.
  4. Bake for one hour, stirring every 15 minutes. Remove from the oven and cool before servirng.
